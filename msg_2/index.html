dyeing message
#2

fabric mordant dyestuffs
linen 100% soymilk ion liquor eucalyptus polyanthemos
date 2026.01.31
dyeing techniques botanical dyeing
steam about 45min
tools thread, wooden stick, plastic wrap

Cut the linen fabric into matching sizes in pairs. Pre-wash them by soaking in warm water with a small amount of neutral detergent (dish soap) for about 5 minutes. Rinse several times in lukewarm water until no more foam appears.

Wring out the excess water and soak the fabric in 1 liter of soy milk for 30 minutes to 1 hour. Remove the fabric, gently squeeze out the moisture, and let it dry for about half a day. Once completely dry, repeat this process two more times (a total of three cycles).

After completing the soy milk mordanting process, place cut eucalyptus (Populus) leaves on the fabric and cover them with another piece of fabric of the same size. Lay a sheet of plastic wrap over it, then tightly roll everything around a wooden rod, applying firm pressure as you roll. Wrap it once more with plastic wrap, then secure it firmly by binding the rod with strong cotton string.

Finally, place it in a preheated steamer and steam for 40 to 50 minutes.
